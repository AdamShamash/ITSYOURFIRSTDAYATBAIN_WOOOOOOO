FIRST DAY OF WORK TOMORROW

Try to come to the office early. Aim to show up 30 minutes early.

Map the route and add 10 to 15 minutes buffer time.

Make sure your alarm is set early enough to avoid stress.

If you are nervous, call someone you trust before bed.

If you cannot sleep, do something calming like reading or listening to an audiobook.

They do not expect perfection in language or knowledge.

It is okay to ask questions and learn on the job.

You will likely have lots of things to talk about with coworkers.

If you cannot sleep, do not stress about it.

Rest is still helpful even if sleep is imperfect.

Send a simple goodnight message if you have time.

ITS YOUR FIRST DAY

Bring energy and positivity. You set the tone as the intern.

Ask a lot of questions. That is expected in the first weeks.

Check your work with others early on to stay aligned.

Learn names and faces from the org calendar if possible.

Start building connections after meeting people, including lunches or 1 on 1s.

Be yourself. You were hired for a reason and will adjust quickly.
